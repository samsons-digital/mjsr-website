Our Story

Pakistan's First
Ski Resort

Established in 1980 and managed by the Samsons Group of Companies, Malam Jabba Ski Resort is Pakistan's first mountain destination. Perched at 2,804 metres in the Swat Valley, we deliver world-class adventure across every season — from winter skiing to summer ziplines.
